SPA Snippets

Socrates

Teacher of Plato, and creator of the Socratic method. The Socratic method uses a series of questions to lead the one(s) being questioned to the answer they were seeking. He is known as being the founder of moral philosophy.

"Wisdom begins in wonder."

-Socrates

Plato

Plato was both the student of Socrates and the teacher of Aristotle. A very cynical philospher, Plato posited that the observable world was an imperfect copy of the real world, which is known as Platonism's Forms theory.

"The object of knowledge is what exists and its function to know about reality."

-Plato

Aristotle

Aristotle was the student of Plato and a large contributor to almost all fields of study during his time, and still influences many fields even to this day. He often used deductive reasoning in his studies, taking empirical observations to deduce logical explanations.

"The more you know, the more you know you don't know."

-Aristotle
